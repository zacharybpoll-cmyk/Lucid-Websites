The Landscape

LA Is Two Cities on a Bike

Los Angeles splits into two fundamentally different cycling worlds. Knowing which one you live in shapes every bike decision you'll make.

Flat Zones — Any Bike Works

  • 🌊 Westside: Santa Monica, Venice, Culver City, Mar Vista, Playa Vista
  • 🏙️ South LA: Leimert Park, Crenshaw, Inglewood, Hawthorne
  • 🌆 The Valley: Reseda, Van Nuys, North Hollywood, Burbank
  • 🏢 Central: Downtown LA, Koreatown, Palms

Hilly Zones — E-Bike Strongly Advised

  • ⛰️ Eastside: Silver Lake, Echo Park, Los Feliz, Highland Park, Eagle Rock
  • 🦅 Mount Washington — grades reach 12%+
  • 🌄 Hollywood Hills, Laurel Canyon, Brentwood
  • 🌊 Pacific Palisades, Cheviot Hills, Elysian Park

Protection Checklist

  • ✅ Kryptonite Evolution Mini U-lock ($60–80) through frame + rear wheel
  • ✅ Secondary chain through front wheel ($30–60)
  • ✅ Lock to staple rack — not signposts (thieves use socket wrenches)
  • ✅ Register free at bikeindex.org/lapd (3× better recovery)
  • ✅ Hide Apple AirTag in handlebars or seatpost
  • ✅ Never leave outside overnight in DTLA, Koreatown, Hollywood
  • ✅ Verify your renters/homeowners insurance covers bike theft

Highest Theft Areas

Keeping It Running

Maintenance for LA's Environment

Salt air at the coast, intense UV, and heat create unique maintenance demands that can be catastrophic for e-bike batteries and silent for mechanical components until it's too late.

🌊 Coastal Salt Air Protocol

Within ~1 mile of the coast, chemical corrosion rates roughly double due to salt + heat combination.

  • 🚿 Rinse with fresh water after every ride — chain, cassette, derailleur, brake hardware
  • 💨 Dry thoroughly — never let water sit in cable housing
  • 🔧 Lube chain every 100–150 miles (vs. 200–250 inland)
  • 🛡️ Apply Boeshield T9 to chain and exposed metal for corrosion barrier
  • 🔩 Check and grease all bolts every 3–4 months — they seize
  • 🏠 Store indoors whenever possible

☀️ Heat & UV Damage

LA's UV index and summer temps cause accelerated breakdown of rubber, plastic, and cable housing.

  • 🔍 Inspect tires every 6 months for cracking and sidewall brittleness
  • 🏠 Store away from direct sunlight — UV accelerates all rubber
  • 🔋 Never leave e-bike battery in a hot car — lithium degrades above 113°F permanently
  • ⏰ Let battery cool before charging after a hot ride
  • 🧴 Apply 303 Aerospace UV protectant to synthetic saddle and rubber components

Maintenance Schedule

IntervalTask
Every rideQuick tire pressure check (LA's rough roads cause faster pressure loss)
Every 2–3 weeks (coastal) / Monthly (inland)Rinse frame, re-lube chain
