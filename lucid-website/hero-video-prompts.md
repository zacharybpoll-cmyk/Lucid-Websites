# Lucid Steel Hero Video Prompts — Gemini Veo 3.1

Three prompts for 7-second hero videos. Dark navy/steel blue palette, clean minimalism, premium wellness aesthetic.

---

## Prompt 1 — "The Sound Wave Reveal" (Science + Beauty)

Cinematic slow-motion close-up of a person speaking naturally into the soft glow of a MacBook screen in a dim, minimalist room. As they speak, translucent steel-blue sound waves emanate from their mouth and flow toward the screen, morphing mid-air into elegant floating data visualizations — glowing numbers, smooth line charts, and radial wellness scores. The color palette is deep navy and cool steel blue against warm skin tones. The waves pulse gently with each syllable. Camera slowly pushes in. Shallow depth of field. The mood is intimate, scientific, and quietly powerful — like watching something invisible become visible for the first time. No text overlays. Anamorphic lens flare. 7 seconds.

---

## Prompt 2 — "The Missing Layer" (Body + Mind Stack)

A person's wrist wearing a sleek fitness tracker slowly rises into frame against a clean dark navy background. Soft holographic health metrics float around the wrist — heart rate, sleep stages, steps — all in warm white light. The camera tilts up to the person's face as they begin to speak. The moment their lips move, a new layer of cool steel-blue data streams appear around their head and throat — calm score, stress level, mood — flowing like luminous ribbons. The two data layers (warm body metrics below, cool voice metrics above) merge into a single unified glow around the person. Cinematic lighting. Shallow depth of field. Atmosphere of quiet revelation. No text. 7 seconds.

---

## Prompt 3 — "25 Seconds" (Speed + Intimacy)

Extreme close-up of a person's lips and jawline in warm, soft side-lighting against a dark background. They're mid-conversation — relaxed, natural, unposed. Visible subtle micro-movements of the throat and jaw as they speak. The camera very slowly orbits around their profile. After a beat, thin luminescent steel-blue particle streams begin tracing along the contours of their jaw and throat, like the voice itself is being mapped in real-time — delicate, precise, beautiful. The particles coalesce into a small, elegant radial score that floats near their chin, pulsing gently. The feeling is: your voice already knows. Moody, intimate, cinematic. Film grain. No text overlays. 7 seconds.

---

## Production Notes

- All three avoid text overlays — the video sits behind/beside website copy
- Color guidance: "the blue of a deep ocean at twilight" for steel blue, "near-black navy" for dark tones
- Style references: Oura Ring website hero, Apple Vision Pro reveal video, Whoop 4.0 product film
- Audio: Designed to be muted (autoplaying on website), visual storytelling is self-contained
