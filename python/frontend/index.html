      Frequently Asked Questions

      Why doesn't the speech timer exactly match how long I talk? ▼

      Speaker verification checks every ~1.2 seconds to ensure only YOUR voice is being captured. This privacy-first approach means some speech at the boundaries of each segment isn't counted. The timer shows verified speech only — not all detected audio.

      Press ⌘+Shift+D to open the Speaker Verification panel and watch verification happening in real time. You can also go to Settings → Voice Profile and enhance your voice profile to improve matching accuracy.

      How does the analysis work? When does it trigger? ▼

      Once 30 seconds of verified speech accumulates, a grace period begins. Attune keeps collecting up to 60 seconds, then auto-triggers the analysis. If you stop speaking between 30-60 seconds, the analysis auto-triggers after 15 seconds of silence.

      What is the Health Score? ▼

      Your daily wellness score from 0-100, calculated each morning from yesterday's voice data. It combines seven voice-based scores — stress, wellbeing, calmness, activation, depression risk, anxiety risk, and emotional stability — weighted by validation strength, plus your calm-time recovery ratio. Think of it as your vocal wellness GPA.

      How does Attune protect my privacy? ▼

      100% on-device. Zero bytes leave your Mac. There are no cloud servers, no data collection, and no third parties. The full 244M-parameter AI model runs locally. Your raw voice audio is never stored — only the derived wellness scores are kept in a local database.

      What are Rhythm Rings? ▼

      Three daily goals displayed as concentric rings: minutes of speech (Speak), minutes in the calm zone (Calm), and number of check-ins. Try to close all three rings each day — like Apple's Activity Rings but for vocal wellness.

      What is the Zone Bar? ▼

      A color-coded timeline showing your stress zones throughout the day. Green = Calm, Yellow = Steady, Orange = Tense, Red = Stressed. It gives you an at-a-glance view of how your day has unfolded emotionally.

      How does voice analysis detect well-being? ▼

      Attune uses the Kintsugi DAM model — a 244M-parameter transformer trained on 35,000 diverse subjects. It was clinically validated in a 14,898-participant study published in Annals of Family Medicine (2025), achieving 71.3% sensitivity and 73.5% specificity from just 25 seconds of natural speech.

      What is calibration and why does it take a few days? ▼

      Attune needs about 3 days of voice data to build your personal baseline. Everyone's voice is different — what sounds "stressed" for you might be "normal" for someone else. Calibration learns YOUR patterns so scores are personalized, not generic.

      Can Attune hear other people's voices? ▼

      No. Attune uses ECAPA-TDNN speaker verification to check every ~1.2-second audio segment against your enrolled voice profile. Segments that don't match you are rejected. This means coworkers on calls, TV audio, and other speakers are filtered out.

      What are Echoes and Compass? ▼

      Echoes are pattern discoveries — insights Attune finds in your data over time (e.g., "Your stress peaks on Wednesdays"). They appear after 7+ days of use. Compass is your weekly wellness direction — ascending, holding, or descending — updated every Monday with a personal intention you can set.
