# Lucid Creator Affiliate Program

## Program Overview

Turn vocal professionals into authentic ambassadors. The program prioritizes genuine product belief over promotional volume — only partner with people who've used Lucid and found it valuable.

---

## Commission Structure

| Parameter | Standard Tier | Power Tier ($5K+/mo referred) | Elite Tier ($15K+/mo referred) |
|-----------|--------------|-------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| **Commission rate** | 20% recurring | 25% recurring | 30% recurring |
| **Duration** | 12 months from each referral's signup | 12 months | 12 months |
| **Cookie window** | 60 days | 60 days | 60 days |
| **Minimum payout** | $50 | $50 | $50 |
| **Payout frequency** | Monthly (Net 30) | Monthly (Net 30) | Monthly (Net 15) |
| **Payment method** | PayPal / direct deposit | PayPal / direct deposit | PayPal / direct deposit |

### Commission Examples

At $9.99/month subscription:
- **10 active referrals**: $19.98/month passive income (Standard 20%)
- **50 active referrals**: $99.90/month (Standard) or $124.88/month (Power 25%)
- **200 active referrals**: $399.60/month (Standard) or $599.40/month (Elite 30%)

Commissions are **recurring** — creators earn every month their referrals stay subscribed, for 12 months per referral.

## Creator Requirements

### Must-Haves
- [ ] Must actually use Lucid for 2+ weeks before promoting
- [ ] Must genuinely believe the product is useful (we will ask)
- [ ] Must disclose the affiliate relationship per FTC guidelines
- [ ] At least 1,000 followers on primary platform (flexible for high-engagement small accounts)

### Content Guidelines
- **DO**: Share honest opinions, show real data, create authentic content
- **DON'T**: Make medical claims, guarantee health outcomes, use fear-based messaging
- **DO**: Adapt messaging to your audience and style
- **DON'T**: Read from a script we provide (we don't provide scripts)

### Minimum Activity
- At least 1 mention per month to maintain active partnership status
- If inactive for 60+ days, partnership moves to dormant (commissions still honored for existing referrals, but no new discount codes)

## Onboarding Flow

### Step 1: Product Trial (Week 1-4)
1. Creator receives free Lucid access
2. Uses product genuinely for 2-4 weeks
3. No promotional obligation during trial period

### Step 2: Feedback Call (Week 3-4)
1. 15-minute call with founder
2. Honest feedback on product experience
3. If creator is enthusiastic → proceed to partnership offer
4. If creator is lukewarm → thank them, no hard feelings, keep their free access

### Step 3: Partnership Setup (Day 1 of partnership)
1. Sign simple affiliate agreement (1-page, plain language)
2. Generate discount code and referral link
3. Send brand assets kit
4. Add to creator Slack channel / email list

### Step 4: Ongoing
1. Monthly earnings report
2. Quarterly check-in call (optional)
3. Early feature access notifications
4. Amplify their content on Lucid's channels (with permission)

## Legal / Compliance

### FTC Disclosure Requirements
All creators must clearly disclose the affiliate relationship. Accepted formats:
- Video: verbal disclosure ("I'm a paid partner of Lucid" or "affiliate link in bio")
- Post caption: "#ad" or "#partner" or "affiliate link" near the top (not buried)
- Bio: "Lucid affiliate" or similar permanent disclosure

### Affiliate Agreement (Key Terms)
- Non-exclusive (creators can promote competitors)
- Either party can terminate with 30 days notice
- Commissions on existing referrals honored through their 12-month window even after termination
- No medical claims, no income guarantees
- Creator owns their content; Lucid may reshare with permission
