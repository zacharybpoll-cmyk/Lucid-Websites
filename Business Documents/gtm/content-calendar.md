# Content Calendar — Lucid Voice Wellness

## Platform Strategy

| Platform | Role | Frequency | Format |
|----------|------|-----------|--------|
| **TikTok** | Primary discovery | 3-5x/week | 30-60s vertical video |
| **Instagram** | Retention + depth | 3-5x/week (repurpose) | 60-70% Reels, 20-30% Carousels, 10% Stories |
| **LinkedIn** | Founder brand / B2B | 2-3x/week | Personal posts (NOT company page) |

**Cross-posting rule**: Create for TikTok first, repurpose to Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts. Never post identical captions — adapt tone for each platform.

## Content Pillars

### 1. Voice Science / Edutainment (40% of content)
Quick, surprising facts about voice and health. The "did you know?" hook.

**Example topics:**
- "3 things your voice reveals about your stress level"
- "Why your voice sounds different when you're tired — and what it means"
- "Your vocal cords move 100-1000x per second. Here's what happens when they're strained."
- "The #1 habit that damages your voice (it's not yelling)"
- "What 'vocal fry' actually does to your vocal cords"
- "Why teachers lose their voice more than any other profession"

### 2. Myth-Busting (20% of content)
Challenge common assumptions. These perform well on TikTok because they create tension.

**Example topics:**
- "Clearing your throat is actually making it worse. Here's why."
- "Whispering doesn't rest your voice — it strains it MORE"
- "Drinking water doesn't directly hydrate your vocal cords"
- "Honey and lemon tea? Here's what actually helps vocal recovery"
- "You don't need to 'warm up' your voice the way you think"

### 3. App Demos & Data Stories (20% of content)
Show Lucid in action — real data, real insights.

**Example topics:**
- "I tracked my voice for 30 days. Here's what I found."
- "My voice strain spikes every Tuesday — here's why" (meeting day)
- "Watch what happens to my vocal metrics after a workout"
- "How my voice changes throughout the day — the data is wild"
- "I was getting sick and my voice detected it before I felt symptoms"

### 4. Founder Story / Behind-the-Scenes (15% of content)
Build connection and trust. People buy from people.

**Example topics:**
- "Why I quit [X] to build a voice health app"
- "The science that convinced me voice is the next vital sign"
- "Building in public: this week's wins and fails"
- "A voice wellness company just shut down after raising $28M. Here's what we're doing differently."
- "What our first 100 users taught us"

### 5. User Stories / Social Proof (5% of content)
Only after you have real users. Never fake these.

**Example topics:**
- "This singer caught early vocal strain before a major performance"
- "A teacher's 30-day vocal health transformation"
- Repost/duet creator content that mentions Lucid

## Production Notes

### TikTok Best Practices
- **Hook in first 1-3 seconds** — the scroll-stopping moment decides everything
- Text overlays are mandatory (most viewers watch without sound initially)
- Trending sounds boost reach but only if relevant
- Post between 7-9am or 6-9pm local time (highest engagement windows)
- Reply to comments with video responses (algorithm loves this)

### Instagram Reels
- Slightly longer captions work (IG users read more than TikTok)
- Add 5-10 relevant hashtags (don't overdo it — IG now penalizes hashtag spam)
- Reels can be up to 90s but 30-60s performs best
- Cross-promote Reels in Stories for extra reach

### Instagram Carousels
- First slide = hook (treat it like a thumbnail)
- Last slide = CTA ("Save this for later" / "Follow for more")
- 5-7 slides is the sweet spot
- Educational carousels get 2.14x more reach than single images

### LinkedIn Personal Posts
- First line is everything (the "see more" fold)
- Personal stories outperform pure information
- Use line breaks aggressively (improves readability on mobile)
- Tag relevant people sparingly (only when genuinely relevant)
- No hashtags or max 3 — LinkedIn hashtags are nearly dead
