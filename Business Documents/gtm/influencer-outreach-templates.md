## Outreach Rules

1. **NEVER send generic templates.** Every email must reference specific content they've made.
2. **Lead with value** (free product), not with the ask (promotion).
3. **Let them use the product first** (2-4 weeks minimum) before proposing the partnership.
4. **Don't chase.** One follow-up max. If no response after 2 touches, move on.
5. **Track everything.** Log outreach dates, responses, and status in `influencer-targets.md`.
6. **Personalization beats volume.** 10 deeply personalized emails > 50 templates.
