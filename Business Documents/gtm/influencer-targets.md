## Status Definitions

| Qualified | Meets criteria (follower count, engagement, content fit) |
| Contacted | Initial outreach sent |
| Responded | They replied (positive, negative, or neutral) |

| Dormant | Was active, now inactive 60+ days |

---

## Research Process (For Each Target)

1. **Find**: Search TikTok/Instagram/YouTube by niche keywords
2. **Screen**: Check follower count (5K-50K sweet spot), engagement rate, content relevance
3. **Deep dive**: Watch 5-10 recent posts. Do they talk about voice care/health? Are comments engaged?
4. **Contact info**: Check bio for email, website, or booking link. If DM-only, note that.
5. **Personalize**: Note 2-3 specific content pieces to reference in outreach
6. **Log**: Fill in the row above with all details
