## Execution Timeline

### Month 1: Foundation (Current)
- Create TikTok + Instagram accounts
- Batch-create 10-15 content pieces
- Post 3x/week TikTok, repurpose to Instagram Reels
- Build list of 50 target micro-influencers
- Set up affiliate tracking (Rewardful, $49/mo)
- Create discount codes + commission structure

### Month 2: Seed & Outreach
- Personalized outreach to 30 micro-influencers with free product
- Continue content cadence with performance data
- Launch referral program for existing users
- LinkedIn founder posts 2-3x/week
- $10-25/day paid boost on top TikTok/Reels

### Month 3: Optimize & Scale
- Double down on top-performing influencers
- Expand to next 30 influencers
- Launch free "voice health check" web tool
- Start SEO blog targeting vocal health keywords
- Evaluate TikTok vs Instagram — shift resources

### Months 4-6: Scale What Works
- Increase paid social on winners ($50-100/day)
- Explore podcast sponsorships in vocal/singing niche
- Consider Product Hunt launch
- Begin B2B outreach if DTC shows PMF
